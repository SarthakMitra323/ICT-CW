Important Internet Ethics Concepts

Digital Citizenship

Responsible use of technology and appropriate, responsible behavior when using digital tools and platforms.

Cyberbullying

The use of electronic communication to bully a person, typically by sending intimidating or threatening messages.

Privacy

The right to control your personal information and how it's collected, used, and shared online.

Netiquette

The set of rules for behaving properly and politely online, including in emails, forums, and social media.

Copyright

Legal rights that protect original works, including text, images, music, and videos from unauthorized use.

Plagiarism

Using someone else's work or ideas without proper attribution or permission.
